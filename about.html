About us

At Urban Side, we're all about infusing a vibrant touch of African culture into mordern, ready-to-wear fashion. Formerly known as Retronow Wears, we've refreshed our identity to embrace the beauty of Africentric fashion.

Our Vision

At Urban Side, we're all about infusing a vibrant touch of African culture into mordern, ready-to-wear fashion. Formerly known as Retronow Wears, we've refreshed our identity to embrace the beauty of Africentric fashion.

Our Mission

To empower you to express your unique style through clothing that effortlessly blends casual comfort, corporate sophistication, traditional flair, contemporary trends, and everyday inspirations. From street to office, our clothes are designed to make you feel confident, stylish, and connected to your roots.

Who We Are

Where Afrocentric style meets everyday chic!

Whether you're rocking a bold Ankara blazer at work or a chic Kente dress for a night out, Urban Side celebrates the diversity and beauty of Afrocentric fashion.
